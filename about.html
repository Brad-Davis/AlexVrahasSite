Howdy! I am an LA-based filmmaker/video editor/animator with a penchant for the ridiculous. Inspired to pursue filmmaking by the emergence of new media on the internet, I have always jumped into the craft with an independent mindset and a passion for bringing niche voices into the spotlight.

I graduated from New York University with a B.F.A. in Film and Television, focusing on post-production, and have had professional experiences with various xlall production companies and independent film projects across NYC.

I currently work as a freelance video editor/animator, as well as create absurd content for my internet-based alias “puhmpkin.”
